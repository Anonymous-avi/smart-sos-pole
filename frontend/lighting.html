LDR Smart Street Light

Automatic street lighting that responds to daylight conditions.

The LDR sensor continuously reads ambient light intensity. In bright daylight, the street lamp remains OFF to save power. At low-light/night conditions, the lamp turns ON automatically for safer roads and footpaths.

How It Works

  1. LDR senses surrounding light level.
  2. Controller compares reading with threshold.
  3. Day mode: light remains OFF.
  4. Night mode: light switches ON automatically.
